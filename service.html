Stop Gap/Bridge Financing

We are experts in bridge financing. They are short term loans typically must be repaid wholesome or in instalment within 1 -4 months. Our loans are made readily available to keep a business running, a personal emergency sorted.

Salary loans/advance

Salary advance is a cash bridging facility against your next pay cheque. It is designed to help cover small but urgent unexpected expenses between pay days. Salary advance’s repayment is 1-2 months instalment or paid in wholesome after a month.

Quick/emergency loans

Our quick loan is a form of short-term personal credit, built for speed and convenience. These products are designed to provide fast solutions for cash emergencies. With flexible repayment plan. Duration is for 1-2 months
